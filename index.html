About RestoNav

Experience the convenience of Restaurant-Hunting at the Comfort of your home. Without the hassle of the traffic! Nothing will ever be the same again.

This website was powered with pure Javascript and can run on virtually any Browser.

Features

Check these outstanding Features!

  • Excellent restaurants all around Cebu and Mandaue Cities handpicked just for you!
  • Each restaurant has a food spcialty so you'll know when it hits your favorites list
  • You can filter which type of restaurants appear on the navigation area.
  • You can see how many customers have visited the restaurant. So you'll see which one is more popular!
  • Draw a rectangle by clicking on the rectangle panel and see how many restaurants are selected.
  • Get directions to your favorite restaurant by setting an origin point (right click) and clicking on "Get Directions" in the info window.
